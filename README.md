# 🗡️ ZEUS SHADOW ASSASSIN — Premium Edition

**A turn-based assassin duel game. Strike from the shadows. Rule the night.**

Challenge Zeus, the Shadow Master, in a tactical duel of precision and power. Every move affects your **Assassin IQ Rating**. Climb the ranks from **Shadow → Blade → Phantom → Reaper → Legend**.

---

## ⚡ Features

- **🧠 Assassin IQ System** — Every smart play, block, and combo boosts your IQ
- **🎖️ 5 Rank Tiers** — Shadow → Blade → Phantom → Reaper → Legend
- **🗡️ 6 Unique Moves** — Quick Strike, Shadow Slash, Assassin's Strike, Shadow Veil, Combo Chain, Shadow Step
- **🔥 Combo System** — Chain consecutive hits for damage multipliers
- **🌙 Stealth Mechanics** — Vanish into shadows to evade and recover
- **🤖 Zeus AI** — Adaptive enemy that counters your strategy
- **🎨 Canvas Arena** — Real-time character rendering with visual effects
- **📜 Shadow Notes** — Built-in notepad for strategies (saved in browser)
- **📊 Full Statistics** — Win rate, damage, perfect duels, smart plays
- **⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts** — 1-6 for moves, N=notes, R=reset, M=menu
- **📱 Fully Responsive** — Desktop, tablet, and mobile

## 🎮 How to Play

### Objective
Reduce Zeus's HP to 0 before he reduces yours.

### Moves

| Key | Move | Damage | Energy | Effect |
|-----|------|--------|--------|--------|
| `1` | Quick Strike | 15 | 0 | Basic attack |
| `2` | Shadow Slash | 35 | 20 | Heavy damage |
| `3` | Assassin's Strike | 60 | 40 | Devastating finisher |
| `4` | Shadow Veil | 0 | 0 | Block +30 Energy |
| `5` | Combo Chain | 50 | 35 | Two-hit combo, builds combo |
| `6` | Shadow Step | 0 | 0 | Evade +50 Energy |

### Combo System
Each consecutive hit builds your combo counter. Higher combo = higher damage multiplier.

### Energy Management
- Quick Strike costs 0 Energy
- Shadow Veil & Shadow Step recover Energy
- You recover 8 Energy automatically each turn

### Rank System

| IQ Range | Rank |
|----------|------|
| 0-59 | 🌙 Shadow |
| 60-99 | 🗡️ Blade |
| 100-139 | 👻 Phantom |
| 140-179 | 💀 Reaper |
| 180-200 | 👑 Legend |
